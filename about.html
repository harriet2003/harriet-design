Meet the designer

Hi, I'm Harriet!

I have always loved the creative field, and for as long as I can remember, been drawing, crafting and creating. From handmade birthday cards and bedazzled notebooks, my skills have transferred to a more digital world since discovering a passion for design.

Studying a Bachelor of Media Design at Media Design School in Auckland unlocked my love for UX/UI design. I learnt many things throughout my studies, but one key takeaway was the importance of connection - with clients, colleagues and consumers to create beautiful experiences that are purposeful and encourage positive change in the world.

Services Include

UX/UI Design, Web Design, Brand Identity, Graphic Design, Social Media Design, Data Visualisation, Campaign Design
