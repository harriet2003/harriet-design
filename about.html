A little about me

Hi, I'm Harriet, an Auckland-based designer specialising in UX and UI. I design with empathy, connecting with people through play and thoughtful design. I am passionate about creating beautiful and purposeful experiences that encourage positive change in the world.

I have always loved the creative field, and for as long as I can remember, been drawing, crafting and creating. From handmade birthday cards and bedazzled notebooks, my skills have transferred to a more digital world since discovering a passion for design.

When I'm not sitting at my desk (or bed) you can find me getting creative other ways with pen and paper. I am most happy by the ocean, love traveling, good food, reading and taking my dog Clemmie for walks.

Services Include

UX/UI Design , Web Design, Brand Identity, Graphic Design, Social Media Design, Data Visualisation, Campaign Design, Visual Art
