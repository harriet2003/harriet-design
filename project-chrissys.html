Chrissy's

A crafty and playful brand identity for an artisan craft business, hand-made with love.

2025

Brand Identity Graphic Freelance
The Challenge

Chrissy's is an artisan craft brand that makes colourful handmade bags and gifts to sell at craft markets. The owner approached me wanting a refreshed brand consisting of a logo, colour palette and some designs for labels and business cards. The style had to reflect the warmth and playfulness of the owner and the beautiful gifts she makes.
